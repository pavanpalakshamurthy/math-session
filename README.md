# Math Champions — Fractions & LCM

A single-page math quiz for advanced 2nd graders covering **Fractions** and **LCM** concepts through word problems.

## Questions
| Level  | Count | Topics |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Easy   | 5     | Basic fractions — identifying parts of a whole |
| Medium | 10    | Adding/subtracting fractions, LCM of 2 numbers, fractions of whole numbers |
| Hard   | 15    | Multi-step fraction problems, LCM of 2–4 numbers, mixed fraction & LCM reasoning |

## Publishing to GitHub Pages

1. Push this repo to GitHub.
2. Go to **Settings → Pages**.
3. Under *Source*, select **Deploy from a branch** → `main` / `master`, folder `/` (root).
4. Save. Your page will be live at `https://<username>.github.io/<repo-name>/`.
